December 8, 1998 
LAWRENCE TECH TEAM WINS 1998 MATH CHALLELNGE!
The 1998 M.A.T.H. Challenge was held on November 7, 1998. Teams of 
3 students from 27 institutions in Michigan (26 colleges and one high 
school) took a challenging 3-hour 10-question exam.
When the results were in, it was the LTU1 team who came out on top, besting 
traditional powerhouses like Hope and Calvin!
The LTU Team 1 score was 56 out of 100, with runners up Hope1 with 53 and Alma2 
and Calvin1 tied with 49.  LTU's Team 2 scored 21, to tie for 19th place, a good
showing for a team with two students new to competitions.
Students on the LTU1 team were Bill Mance, Scott Ligon, and Ken Kopp.
and on the LTU2 team were Carl Yute, Bill Kolasa, and Steve Holcomb.
Coach Ruth Favro is very proud of the great work done by all the students, 
who worked very hard at problem sessions before the exam. We are looking 
forward to the Lower Michigan Math Competition (LMMC) in the Spring, 
where we will meet some of these same teams again.
